About Toppings

Wolverton's Local Takeaway

Toppings serves hand-pressed angus burgers, loaded hot dogs and a full menu of oriental street food bites — burgers on one side, wontons and gyoza on the other, all cooked fresh to order from Mabel Road. Rated 4.8 stars from over 120 Uber Eats reviews, with a full vegetarian and vegan menu alongside the classics.
